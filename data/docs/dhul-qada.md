# Dhu'l-Qa'da

**Dhu'l-Qa'da**[a] ([Arabic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language): ‏ذُو ٱلْقَعْدَة‎, *Ḏū l-Qaʿda*, IPA: [ðu‿l.qaʕ.da]) is the eleventh month in the [Islamic calendar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_calendar).

It could possibly mean "possessor or owner of the sitting and seating place" - the space occupied while sitting or the manner of the sitting, pose or posture.

It is one of the four [sacred months](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sacred_months) in Islam during which warfare is prohibited, hence the name "Master of [Truces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Truce)".

## Islamic events

- **5 AH** — the Muslims took part in the [Battle of the Trench](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_the_Trench).
- **6 AH** — [Truce of Hudaybiya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Hudaybiya).
- **6 AH** — [Pledge of the Tree](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pledge_of_the_Tree).
- **7 AH** — [The first pilgrimage](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_first_pilgrimage) - the return to [Mecca](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mecca) for the performance of [Umrah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umrah) by [Muhammad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad) and his companions.
- **1 Dhu'l-Qa'da** — birth anniversary of [Fatima bint Musa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fatima_bint_Musa).
- **1 Dhu'l-Qa'da** — [Treaty of Hudaybiya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Hudaybiya).
- **8 Dhu'l-Qa'da** — [Hajj](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hajj) was made incumbent upon Muslims in 8 AH.
- **11 Dhu'l-Qa'da** — birth anniversary of [Imam Ali ibn Musa al-Rida](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_al-Rida), the eighth [Twelver Imam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imamate_(Twelver_doctrine)).
- **23 Dhu'l-Qa'da** — martyrdom of Imam Ali al-Rida according to one tradition.
- **25 Dhu'l-Qa'da** — [Arabic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language): يوم دحو الارض, the day earth was laid beneath the [Ka'ba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ka'ba), and the birth date of [Ibrahim](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abraham_in_Islam) and [Jesus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jesus_in_Islam).
- **29 Dhu'l-Qa'da** — martyrdom of [Imam Muhammad ibn Ali at-Taqi al-Jawad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad_al-Jawad), the ninth Twelver Imam.
