# Rabi I

**Rabi I** ([Arabic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language): ‏رَبِيع الْأَوَّل‎, romanized: Rabīʿ al-ʾAwwal,[a] lit. 'Rabi the First')[b] is the third month of the [Islamic calendar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_calendar). The name *Rabīʿ al-ʾAwwal* means 'the first [spring](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spring_(season))', referring to its position in the [pre-Islamic Arabian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pre-Islamic_Arabia) calendar.

## Islamic events

- **1 Rabi I 897 AH** — the fall of the [Emirate of Granada](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emirate_of_Granada), the final Muslim kingdom of [al-Andalus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Andalus)
- **8 Rabi I** — death of Imam Hassan Al-Askari [Twelver Imām](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twelvers), [Hasan al-‘Askarī](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hasan_al-Askari) (*see: Chup Tazia*)
- **9 Rabi I** — [Eid e shuja](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eid_e_shuja)
- **12 Rabi I** — Sunni Muslims observe [Mawlid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mawlid) in commemoration of [Muhammad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad)'s birthday
- **13 Rabi I** — Death of [Umm Rubab](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rubab_bint_Imra_al-Qais) (beloved wife of Imam Hussain)
- **17 Rabi I** — [Shia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shia) celebrate the birthday of the [Imām](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imām) [Ja‘far al-Sādiq](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ja'far_al-Sadiq).
- **18 Rabi I** — birth of [Umm Kulthum bint Ali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umm_Kulthum_bint_Ali)
- **26 Rabi I 1333 AH** — death of [Khwaja Sirajuddin Naqshbandi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khwaja_Sirajuddin_Naqshbandi), a [Naqshbandi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Naqshbandi) Sufi shaykh
- The [Hijra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hijra_(Islam)) (migration) took place in this month
- Eid-e-Zahra (a.k.a. [Eid e shuja](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eid_e_shuja)), a celebration of [Shi‘ah Muslims](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shia)
- Marriage of [Muhammad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad) to [Khadijah bint Khuwaylid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khadijah_bint_Khuwaylid)
- Building of the [Quba Mosque](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quba_Mosque) (first mosque in Islam)
- The week including 12th and 17th is called [Islamic Unity Week](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mawlid#Islamic_Unity_Week) in [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran) to address both Sunni and Shia views on the birth date of [Muhammad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad).[6]
