# Safar

**Safar** ([Arabic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language): صَفَر, romanized: Ṣafar), also spelt as **Safer** in [Turkish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkish_language),[1] is the second month of the lunar [Islamic calendar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_calendar).

Most of the Islamic months were named according to ancient Sabean/Sabaic weather conditions; however, since the calendar is lunar, the months shift by about 11 days every solar year, meaning that these conditions do not necessarily correspond to the name of the month.

## Islamic events

- **01 Safar 61 AH** — prisoners of [Karbalā](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Karbala) entered [Yazid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yazid_I)'s Palace in Syria
- **10 Safar 117 AH** — death of [Sakina bint Husayn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sakina_bint_Husayn), youngest daughter of [Hussain ibn Ali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hussain_ibn_Ali) and a prisoner of [Karbalā](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Karbala)
- **17  Safar 202 AH** — martyrdom of [Ali al-Ridha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_al-Ridha) according to one tradition
- **18 Safar** — Grand Magal pilgrimage is celebrated at [Touba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Touba), [Senegal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senegal), commemorating the departure of [Cheikh Ahmadou Bamba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amadou_Bamba)
- **18, 19 and 20 Safar** — Death Anniversary ([urs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Urs)) of [Ali Hajveri](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_Hujwiri) is celebrated at [Data Darbar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_Darbar), [Lahore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lahore)
- **Every 20 or 21 Safar** — [Arba'een](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arba'een) or Chehlum (the 40th day after [Ashura](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Day_of_Ashura))
- **27 Safar 1 AH** — Migration was started ([Hijrah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hijrah)) from Mecca to Medina by [Muhammad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad) with [Abu Bakr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abu_Bakr)
- **27 Safar 589 AH** — death of [Salahuddin al-Ayyubi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saladin)
- **28 Safar 11 AH** — [Akhiri Chahar Shambah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akhiri_Chahar_Shambah)
- **28 Safar 50 AH** — Martyrdom of [Imam Hasan ibn ‘Alī](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hasan_ibn_Ali), grandson of Muhammad
