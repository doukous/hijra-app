# Jumada II

**Jumada II** ([Arabic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language): ‏جُمَادَى ٱلثَّانِيَة‎, romanized: Jumādā th-Thāniya,[a] lit. 'Jumada the Second', more commonly called ‏جُمَادَى ٱلْآخِرَة‎, Jumādā l-ʾĀkhira,[b] lit. 'Jumada the Last')[c] is the sixth month of the [Islamic calendar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_calendar). The word *jamād* (‏جماد‎), from which the name of the month is derived, is used to denote dry, parched land, a land devoid of rain. *Jumādā* (جمادى) may also be related to a verb meaning 'to freeze', and another account relates that water would freeze in [pre-Islamic Arabia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pre-Islamic_Arabia) during this time of year.

## Islamic events

- **3 Jumada II** — death of [Muhammad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad)'s daughter [Fatima](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fatima) in 11 AH.
- **3 Jumada II** — death of [Harun al-Rashid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harun_al-Rashid), the fifth [Abbasid caliph](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Abbasid_caliphs).
- **10 Jumada II** — victory of [Ali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali) in the [Battle of the Camel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_the_Camel).
- **13 Jumada II** — death of [Umm al-Banin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umm_al-Banin) (the mother of [Abbas ibn Ali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abbas_ibn_Ali)).
- **20 Jumada II** — birth of [Muhammad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad)'s daughter [Fatima Zahra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fatima_Zahra).
- **22 Jumada II** — death of Caliph [Abu Bakr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abu_Bakr).
- **25 Jumada II of 564 AH** — [Saladin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saladin) became [amir](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amir) of [Egypt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt).
