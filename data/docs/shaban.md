# Sha'ban

**Shaʽban** ([Arabic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language): شَعْبَان *Šaʿbān*) is the eighth month of the [Islamic calendar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_calendar). It is called the month of 'separation', as the word means 'to disperse' or 'to separate' because the [pre-Islamic Arabs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Religion_in_pre-Islamic_Arabia) used to disperse in search of water.[1]

The fifteenth night of this month is [Mid-Sha'ban](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mid-Sha'ban),[2] which coincides with the celebration of [Shab-e-Barat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shab-e-Barat) in Muslim communities all over Asia.

Sha'ban is the last [lunar month](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lunar_month) before [Ramadan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ramadan), and so Muslims determine in it when the first day of Ramadan fasting will be. In the second [Hijri year](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hijri_year) (624 CE), [fasting during Ramadan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fasting_during_Ramadan) was made obligatory during this month.[1]

## Islamic events

- **01 Sha'ban** — birth of [Zaynab bint Ali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zaynab_bint_Ali)
- **02 Sha'ban** — death of (Imam Azam) [Abu Hanifa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abu_Hanifa)
- **03 Sha'ban** — birth of [Husayn ibn Ali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Husayn_ibn_Ali)
- **04 Sha'ban** — birth of [Abbas ibn Ali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abbas_ibn_Ali)
- **05 Sha'ban** — birth of [Ali ibn Husayn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_ibn_Husayn)
- **05 Sha'ban** — death of Fizza, the hand-maiden (Qaneez) of [Fatimah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fatimah)
- **07 Sha'ban** — birth of [Qasim ibn Hasan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qasim_ibn_Hasan)
- **11 Sha'ban** — birth of [Ali al-Akbar ibn Husayn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ali_al-Akbar_ibn_Husayn)
- **15 Sha'ban** — holiday known as [Laylat al-Bara'at](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laylat_al-Bara'at) or Nisfu Sha'ban; birth of [Muhammad al-Mahdi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad_al-Mahdi)
- **21 Sha'ban** — death of [Lal Shahbaz Qalandar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lal_Shahbaz_Qalandar)
- **22 Sha'ban** — death of [Muhammad Usman Damani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad_Usman_Damani)
- **27 Sha'ban** — death of [Sayyad Laal Shah Hamdani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sayyad_Laal_Shah_Hamdani)
